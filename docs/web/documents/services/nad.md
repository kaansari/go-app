# What are the benefits of NAD+?


**NAD+ therapy**, or Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide therapy, involves the administration of Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide, a coenzyme found in all living cells, to potentially enhance various cellular processes. NAD+ plays a crucial role in energy metabolism and is involved in several biological processes, including DNA repair, cell signaling, and gene expression.

Some of the purported benefits of NAD+ therapy include:

  - **Anti-Aging:** Individuals seeking to slow down the aging process and promote overall health and longevity may opt for NAD+ therapy.

  - **Athletes:** Athletes and fitness enthusiasts may use NAD+ therapy to enhance energy levels, improve endurance, and support muscle recovery.

  - **Individuals with Age-Related Conditions:** NAD+ therapy could be beneficial for those experiencing age-related health issues such as cognitive decline, decreased metabolism, or reduced vitality.

  - **Patients with Anxiety, PTSD, or Neurological Disorders:** NAD+ therapy has been explored for its potential neuroprotective effects, making it relevant for individuals with neurodegenerative disorders like Alzheimer's or Parkinson's disease.

  - **Stop being addicted to legal and illegal drugs:** NAD+ therapy is sometimes used as part of substance abuse treatment programs to help manage withdrawal symptoms and support recovery.

  - **General Wellness:** People looking to optimize their overall health and well-being may incorporate NAD+ therapy into their wellness routines to boost cellular health, improve energy levels, and support various bodily functions.
